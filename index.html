You Can't Grow SEO & AI Visibility by Just Publishing Content.

Publishing more used to be enough. Get the pages live, get them indexed, watch traffic climb.
Not anymore.

Plenty of companies are now stuck in the same loop. They publish more, but organic traffic and visibility keep sliding. Core updates get blamed. AI search gets blamed. A vague “technical issue” gets blamed. So teams do the only thing they know how to do. They publish even more content. And nothing changes.

The real problem is rarely the volume of content.
It's that no one has connected the strategy, the technical foundations, and the implementation into a single plan that actually ships. Recommendations sit in a doc. Dev never prioritises them. Content gets published onto a site that can't support it. The work happens, but the growth doesn't.

That's the gap I close.

I work with companies and agencies that aren't seeing results from the SEO channel, from fast-growing sites to large, complex ones.

I don't just hand over advice. I diagnose what's actually holding growth back, build a prioritised plan around it, and work alongside your dev, product and content teams to get it implemented.

A few examples:

  • For the UK's largest business finance broker, I led the SEO and content strategy into untapped sectors. The result: £8M added to the sales pipeline and a 130% rise in non-brand traffic in 12 months.
  • For a B2B research store, brought in after they'd already worked with several agencies, I found the few issues really holding the site back and worked with their dev team to ship the fixes. That added over £1M in non-brand revenue in 12 months.
  • For an enterprise AI SaaS business in Europe, I identified that there was untapped potential in non-branded SEO traffic. The result: 2x non-branded SEO traffic YoY to the marketing website.
  • A 2 million page meal-plan site buried in low-value pages, I built a framework that reduced their index bloat by -67% which doubled organic traffic from 600K to 1.2M visits a month.

If your content engine is running but visibility still isn't growing, the answer usually isn't more content. It's a senior pair of hands who can find the real constraint and work with your team to fix it.

And that is where I can help.
